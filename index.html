620,000 followers.

“modern-day pen

the museum’s Instagram feed and

“This is a
museum that gets

recalls
thinking, one that not
only realizes
mobile photography is
exploding, but
also
understands there is a community

that goes

in

#MobilePhotoNow, #jj

89 countries on six

Kuster
and Johnson did an

650
