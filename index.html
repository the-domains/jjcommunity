How

Photo

Far enough

The

museum’s

“One of the

their

they went

assignments on a

Jennifer

Poleon, who organized

“They gave

would come
back,

the

that

hundreds of
submissions
and had five small

“Here is

not
just
about hanging

about
engagement and it’s
about
community.” He
