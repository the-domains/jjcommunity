Far enough to inspire

“One of
the

Photo
Leaguers did is during their

assignments
on a

photography

Hunts
—

Columbus
exhibition. “They gave themselves a word or a
phrase
and
they
would come
back, develop the pictures and
have a
pop-up
exhibition to go along

took that
concept and modernized it.”
During that
time, they
received hundreds of
submissions and
had
five small
shows. Then in late 2013,
Kevin Kuster, a

Playboy

head

was

this major
museum who gets it, that

not

were in. While
