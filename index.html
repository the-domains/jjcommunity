How

the
Photo
League?
Far enough
to inspire
hundreds
of

people to

Instagram images
at
“Mobile
Photo
Now,”

The

promote social

photography
with their
hand-held
cameras. So it’s

to

today’s smartphones
could
be used

similarly. It was a

evident

during the
museum’s
2012 “Radical

“One of the

Poleon, who organized

“They gave

Then in late 2013, Kevin

with the idea

“the
